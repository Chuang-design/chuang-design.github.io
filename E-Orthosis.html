E-Orthosis

April 2023

Yue Yang, Lei Ren, Chuang Chen, Xinyue Wang, Yitao Fan, Yilin Shao, Kuangqi Zhu, Jiaji Li, Qi Wang, Lingyun Sun, Ye Tao, Guanyun Wang*

Orthoses with electronic functions have emerged as a promising medical product in response to the increasing demand for rehabilitation training, therapy assistance, and health monitoring. However, fabricating this “smart orthosis” often requires long development cycles and exorbitant prices. We introduce E-Orthosis, an integrated fabrication approach with construction toolkits for healthcare professionals to rapidly embed electronics into off-the-shelf orthoses. The system includes modular magnetic interfaces, textile electrodes, and tools for embedding and circuit transfer.

Publication: ACM CHI Conference on Human Factors in Computing Systems 2023， Full Paper
